How Our Model Works
📐 From Linear to Exponential: The Math Behind the Prediction

📊 The Formula

Instead of predicting reaction time directly, we predict its natural logarithm:

log(y) = 0.3189 + β₁x₁ + β₂x₂ + ...

Then convert back using exponential function:

y = elog(y)
Intercept (log scale): 0.3189
Base reaction time (e0.3189): 1.375 seconds

✨ Why Log Transform?

  • Always positive predictions Reaction time can't be negative
  • Handles multiplicative effects Stress doesn't add seconds, it multiplies them
  • Normalizes distribution Makes skewed data more "bell-shaped"
  • Improves model stability Reduces impact of outliers
